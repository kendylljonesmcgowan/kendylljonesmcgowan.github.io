Welcome! My name is Kendyll Jones-McGowan.

I am a student, currently studying at Fleming College. This website is all about my map making journey in post secondary - enjoy!

I am a graduate of Fleming College's Ecosystem Management Technician (EM) program. I completed the program in 2021, but wasn't quite finished with school at that point. During my time in the EM program, I had the opportunity to take three GIS related courses. Through these courses I discovered a deep passion for the GIS field. Before these courses, I had never experienced such passion for any subject. Right away I knew this was the career I desperately wanted to see myself in. I applied to Fleming College's GIS Post-Graduate program - which is where I find myself currently. Please see the Gallery section of this website to enjoy (or cringe at) a few maps made by me.
